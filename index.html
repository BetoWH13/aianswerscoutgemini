Reality note

What “automated” means here

It runs the scout batch.

Fast Scout uses Gemini reasoning. Grounded Search asks Gemini to use Google Search grounding when your account/model supports it.

It does not impersonate every platform.

ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity, and Google AI each have their own behavior. This is automated scouting, not legal-grade platform rank tracking.

It needs one Gemini API key.

True automation requires a server-side API provider. Store GEMINI_API_KEY as a Netlify environment variable, never in browser code.

It is for niche decisions.

Use it to decide what to build, which cities to test, and where weak answer patterns appear. Do not sell it as ranking proof.

AI Answer Scout Gemini is an internal research aid. AI answers vary by time, location, model, account, and platform. Treat outputs as directional intelligence, not proof of ranking, demand, or platform inclusion.
